About Gloheal

An e-health platform powered by Tata Communications

Welcome to Gloheal, where we are revolutionizing the healthcare industry with our e-health clinical management platform and cutting-edge digital solutions. Imagine a world where healthcare is not limited by physical boundaries. Gloheal is here to solve the problem of outreach healthcare facilities by leveraging the power of technology. We solve the challenge of outreach healthcare facilities and address the inefficiencies of information sharing in IPD and OPD processes.

What sets Gloheal apart is our commitment to being the only end-to-end managed platform that connects the dots of the entire healthcare ecosystem. We bring together all the fragmented pieces of the puzzle and create a cohesive and efficient digital solution. From virtual consultations to digital prescriptions, our platform encompasses a comprehensive range of e-health solutions tailored to the needs of the healthcare industry.
